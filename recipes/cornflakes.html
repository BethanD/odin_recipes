Cornflakes

What, I couldn't find a smaller cornflake image? I hate cornflakes and milk.

Description

Unless your name is Homer Simpson, this is just about as easy as it gets.

Ingredients

  • Your every-day, run of the mill cornflakes.
  • Milk (Cow/soy/almond/oat whatever floats your boat - I don't care, I hate milk).

Steps

  1. Add cornflake to your favourite bowl.
  2. Add your milk of choice.
  3. If - and only if - your cornflakes don't immediately catch on fire, eat and enjoy.
